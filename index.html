Odin Recipes

  • Doenjang-jjigae
  • Ceviche

Ingredients

  • 400g fish fillet
  • 250g leche de tigre
  • 100g red onions
  • 1 lime
  • 5g salt
  • 5g ajinomoto glutamate
  • 5g coriander
  • 5g chili cut, no seeds
  • 1 avocado

Instructions

  1. Cut the red onions in julienne (long thin strips) and put them into a bowl with water and ice to lose the aggressive onion flavor and get more crunchy.
  2. Take out all the fish bones from the fish filet and cut the filet into cubes, approximately 1 cm x 1 cm.
  3. Put the fish cubes into a bowl and season with salt, ajinomoto, and lime juice. Mix everything well.
  4. Add the onions, the leche de tigre, and mix again.
  5. Cut the coriander leaves and the chilies (without the seeds), add to the mixture, and stir again.
  6. Plate, and distribute the marinade evenly, so every plate has the same amount of letche the tigre. Cut the avocado in slices and place on the plates.
